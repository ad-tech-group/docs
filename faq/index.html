What is OpenSSP?
OpenSSP is exactly what the name says: an open source Supply Side Platform. Useable for everyone.
[back to top]
What is a Supply Side Platform?
SSP stand for Supply-Side-Platform and is located on the sell side in the RTB Ecosystem
which means it is a technology for publisher to sell their inventory.

It is a automatic auction for adimpressions.
The impressions can be served on mobile or desktop, can deliver video impressions or banner impressions.

The transaction is processed in Real-time, which means the auction is finished within 50-100ms and the ad is delivered.

Is OpenSSP OpenRTB compatible?
Yes of course. OpenRTB is the de facto standard in real-time-advertising. It is fully supported up to version 2.4.
For more information visit iab.
